### 11:00 → 13:00 · Grand Palace + Wat Phra Kaew (Emerald Buddha) 🏛

**Weather** · 28→30°C feels 35→37, light drizzle possible, UV 8–9 climbing. **Peak heat starts at 13:00.** Sunshine returning.

**Cost:** 500 THB pp. **Total: 1,000 THB.** Cash or card at kiosk.

**Dress code (strict, both):** covered shoulders + knees. No shorts, no sleeveless. Sarongs to borrow at gate, but they run out — **wear compliant clothes from hotel.**

**Plan:** Allow 1.5–2 hr. Highlights:

- **Outer wall murals (178 panels)** — depict the Ramakien epic. Walk full perimeter.
- **Phra Si Rattana Chedi** — golden spire, unmissable photo.

⚠️ **Scam alert:** anyone outside the gate telling you "the palace is closed today" and offering an alternative tour — **ignore and walk to the ticket window.** It is open. Tuk-tuk gem-shop scam variant.

**Hydrate:** carry 2 bottles of water. Some shaded areas but courtyard is mostly exposed.

**Alternatives & when to use:**

# Practical · rules · scams

### Heat strategy
- Outdoor activities only **before 11 AM and after 5 PM.**
- During 12–4 PM: malls / hotel / AC cafés.
- Drink **1 bottle of water per hour outdoors** minimum. 7-Eleven is everywhere.
- Sunscreen SPF 50+, reapply every 2 hr outdoors.
- **27 Apr was zenith passage** — late April UV is the highest of the year.

### Rain prep
- 40–76% rain prob across multiple windows. Compact umbrella each.
- Rain in Bangkok is **short and heavy** — if it starts, step into a 7-Eleven or covered walkway for 15 min, it passes.
- **Rule of thumb:** rain probability ≠ rain volume. Check actual mm/hr in forecast:
  - **0–0.5 mm/hr** = drizzle, umbrella OK, walk
  - **0.5–3 mm/hr** = light rain, umbrella + raincoat, slow down
  - **>3 mm/hr** = heavy, stay indoor, wait it out
  - 9 AM Day 2 = 8.4 mm/hr (real avoidance trigger). 14:00 Day 2 = 0.1 mm/hr (drizzle, fine).

### Temple dress code (Grand Palace strictest)
- Covered shoulders + knees (both).
- No shorts, no sleeveless, no tight leggings.
- Sarongs at the gate but they run out — wear compliant clothes from hotel.
- **Remove shoes** before entering halls. Wear easy-off shoes.

### Money
- Get **Rabbit Card** at any BTS station (~200 THB deposit + initial load). Works on BTS and MRT both.
- Markets cash only. Carry small bills.

### Getting around
- BTS + MRT for 90% of the trip.
- Grab for airport and any off-station destination.
- **Avoid metered taxis that won't use the meter** — walk away.
- **Never take stationary tuk-tuks at tourist sites** — they take you to gem shops.

### Scam awareness
- "Grand Palace is closed today" — always false. Walk to ticket window yourself.
- Tuk-tuk "sightseeing tours" — ALWAYS end at a gem shop. Never engage.
- "Long-tail boat tour 1,500 THB" — local fair price is 200–400 THB pp, negotiate or walk.

### Halal-friendly summary
- **Default-safe:** Soi 3 / Soi Nana street, Thipsamai pad Thai (halal certified), Saman Islam at Chatuchak, ICONSIAM SookSiam most stalls, hotel restaurant.
- **Ask before:** Thai street food (cross-contamination with non-halal common). Look for Halal sticker (الحلال) or "ฮาลาล".
- **Vegan-safe** (defaults halal too): mango sticky rice, coconut ice cream, fresh fruit, Thai tea.
- **Non-halal markers** (J6 only): pork dishes (moo ping skewers, moo daeng red roast pork, pork belly), boat noodles (pork blood broth), Jodd Fairs volcano ribs.

### Basic Thai courtesy
- Sawasdee krap (men) / sawasdee ka (women) — hello.
- Khob khun krap / ka — thank you.
- Thao rai — how much.
- **Always smile.** Calm smile gets better service and prices.

### Hotel tips (GLOW Sukhumvit 5)
- **Message GLOW the day before** via Line/email/Booking — request 9 AM early check-in. 24hr front desk responds; often accommodates free if room available, otherwise offers day-room (paid) or comfortable lobby + bag drop.
- **Bag storage Day 3** — standard free service via 24hr desk, ~6 hr.
- **Rooftop pool** ✓ — main amenity upgrade vs lyf. Pool typically 07:00–21:00, confirm at check-in. Use Day 2 14:30–16:00 rest window OR Day 3 morning pre-Chatuchak.
- **Soi 5** = one Soi west of Nana BTS. Same ~5 min walk to Soi 3 halal street as Soi 8 was.
